Intermediate


After successfully passing the Intermediate course, a student is eligible to get admission in bachelor degree program. The subjects in undergraduate degree level are basically dependent on several factors. For example, they may relate to student's personal interests or subjects of intermediate level.

Bachelor


The 4 year bachelor degree is becoming a wise and modern degree option for the students who want to graduate. These programs are not only beneficial for the students due to the weightage they have among educational criterions but also their recognition by the job market.

Post-Graduate


A Masters degree is the specialized postgraduate university degree of any profession or subject. It comes after bachelors and students can only take admission to this program if they have completed and passed out their bachelors.
